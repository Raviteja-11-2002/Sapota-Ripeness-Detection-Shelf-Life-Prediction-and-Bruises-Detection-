About Us

"Sapota Quality Assurance: Ripe for Innovation, Predicting Freshness with Thermal Precision!"

We provide great services

The ripeness detection and shelf life prediction of sapota (Manilkara zapota), commonly known as chikoo or sapodilla, are crucial aspects in ensuring optimal quality and reducing post-harvest losses. This study proposes a novel approach utilizing thermal imaging technology to assess sapota ripeness and predict its shelf life accurately. Thermal imaging offers a non-destructive and rapid method to capture surface temperature variations, which correlate with fruit ripening stages and subsequent deterioration. By analyzing the thermal patterns obtained from sapota samples at different ripeness stages, a robust model can be developed to predict shelf life based on thermal signatures.
